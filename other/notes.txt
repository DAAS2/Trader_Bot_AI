Trader Bot Functions:
- Get past data, around 5 days of data, then  use indicators.py to get all data from there,
- Get current data, then use indicators.py to get all data from there, using yfinance library
- Use trading_view library to get the latest values as well as recommendations of "BUY" or "SELL" or "NEUTRAL"
- Use ta to get the latest candlestick pattern
- Create decision-making where bot will use both ta_data and trading_view data to come to conclusion and provide logical decision
    - Action
    - Reason
    - Estimated Entry - The price at which the bot suggests entering the trade (usually the current close).
    - Estimated Stop Loss - The price level where you would exit the trade to limit potential losses, calculated dynamically based on ATR.
    - Estimated Take Profit - The price level where you would exit the trade to lock in potential gains, also calculated dynamically based on ATR.
    - Candlestick Trend Interpretation -  A short prediction about the immediate future price movement based on the latest candle's shape and historical context.

Definitions of Terms:
- Average directional index (ADX) is a short-term chart indicator. It can be used to help you evaluate the market or an investment's strength. ADX currently suggests the short-term bearish momentum behind stocks appears strong.
- The Relative Strength Index (RSI) is an oscillating indicator that is designed to measure a stock's momentum, which is both the speed and size of price changes. Helps identify if stock is overbought or oversold
- Moving average convergence divergence (MACD), is a trading indicator, which can help measure a stock's momentum and identify potential entries and exits
- The Ulcer Index (UI) is a technical indicator that measures downside risk in terms of both the depth and duration of price declines
- The Bollinger Bands (BB) is a technical analysis tool that consists of a moving average and two standard deviations plotted above and below it. 
  The bands are used to gauge volatility and measure the degree of price movement relative to the average price
